Moong Dal Dhokla

Description

Moong Dal dhokla is a healthy, tasty and nutritious dhokla variety made with whole mung beans, herbs and spices. They have a beany taste and flavor unlike Khaman. This snack is a savory steamed vegan cake that is protein-rich and guilt-free that you can enjoy any time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole moong beans
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-green chilli paste
  • Â½ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt as required

Steps

  1. Soak the moong beans in water for 4-5 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the water and blend the moong beans with ginger-green chilli paste and water to a smooth batter.
  3. Add salt, lemon juice, coriander leaves, and oil to the batter and mix well.
  4. Grease a steaming tray or plate with oil.
  5. Pour the batter into the tray and steam for 15-20 minutes until cooked.
  6. Let it cool for a few minutes, then cut into pieces.
  7. Serve hot with green chutney or sweet chutney.
  8. Enjoy your healthy and delicious Moong Dal Dhokla!
